Mohammad Robiul Islam

I am an MS–PhD integrated researcher in Electronic Engineering at Kwangwoon University, Seoul, South Korea, and a member of the Advanced Sensor and Energy Research (ASER) Laboratory. My research focuses on triboelectric nanogenerators, wearable sensors, smart textiles, flexible electronics, and nanomaterial-based energy harvesting systems for healthcare monitoring, human–machine interaction, and self-powered device applications.

Research

My research interests include triboelectric nanogenerators (TENGs), wearable energy harvesting systems, flexible and stretchable sensors, smart textiles, bioelectronics, and nanocomposite materials. I am particularly interested in developing self-powered wearable platforms that combine sensing, energy conversion, and intelligent data analysis for IoT-based health monitoring and next-generation human-centered electronics.
